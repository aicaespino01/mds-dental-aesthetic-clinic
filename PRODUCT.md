# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Families and individuals in Mabini, Batangas and the surrounding region looking for a comprehensive dental clinic. This spans routine general dentistry patients, families with children needing orthodontic care, and patients seeking specialist treatment (TMJ disorders, sleep & airway issues, myofunctional therapy, oral surgery) or cosmetic/aesthetic dentistry. They're typically comparing local clinics online, checking credentials and range of services, and deciding whether to book, call, or message on Facebook before ever setting foot in the clinic.

## Product Purpose

Establish MDS Dental & Aesthetic Clinic as a credible, full-service clinic — not just a general dentist, but one backed by real specialists (TMJ, sleep & airway dentistry, myofunctional dentistry, registered Invisalign/SmarTee aligner provider) alongside aesthetic dentistry. Success means visitors quickly understand the breadth and credibility of services offered, and can move effortlessly from browsing to booking an appointment, calling, or messaging on Facebook/Messenger.

## Brand Personality

Warm and approachable at the surface — a real neighborhood family clinic, not intimidating — with genuine clinical authority underneath. Specialist credentials should feel earned and visible, not buried in dense text. "Expert care, warmly delivered": avoid both the cold, sterile hospital-brochure look and an impersonal corporate-chain feel.

## Anti-references

- Stock photos of unnaturally white, generic smiles
- Clip-art tooth icons and dental-cliché imagery
- Cluttered grids of identical service icons (icon + heading + text, repeated endlessly)
- Sterile all-white "hospital brochure" aesthetic
- Impersonal, corporate-chain template feel — this is a local, personal clinic

## Design Principles

1. Credibility through specialization — TMJ, sleep & airway, myofunctional dentistry, and Invisalign/SmarTee provider status get real visual weight, not a buried bullet list
2. Multiple easy paths to action — booking form, phone, and Messenger are all treated as first-class contact paths, not one dominant CTA with the rest as afterthoughts
3. Warmth without losing authority — approachable and comforting in tone, but confidence-inspiring, never cutesy
4. Local & personal — reads as a real clinic rooted in Mabini, Batangas, not a franchise template

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline. Generous type size and contrast throughout (patient base skews toward families and older patients). Clear, large tap targets for mobile, since most local traffic is likely to arrive via phone browsing or Messenger.
